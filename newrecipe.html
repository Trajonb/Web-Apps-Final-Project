BLUEBERRY PANCAKE RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 5 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 2 cups of fresh blueberries

Steps to Preparing

  1. In a large bowl whisk the flour, baking powder, and brown sugar together
  2. In separate bowl whisk the eggs, vanilla and milk together
  3. Add the wet ingredients into the dry and mix until just combined. Lastly mix in the melted butter and stir until combined
  4. Set the batter aside while you heat your griddle to medium-low heat
  5. Then scoop out 1/4 cup of pancake batter onto the hot griddle and top evenly with blueberries, as many or few as you prefer
  6. Cook until the edges are set and bubbles form on top of the pancake. Flip and cook until browned
  7. Serve Warm!
